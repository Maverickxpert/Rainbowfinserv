Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum income required for a personal loan?

For salaried individuals, the minimum income is Rs 25,000 per month. For self-employed individuals, it's Rs 15 lakh annually.

How long does loan approval take?

Most loans are approved within 24-48 hours once all documents are submitted and verified.

Can I prepay my loan without penalty?

Yes, home loans have no prepayment penalty. For other loans, terms may vary.
